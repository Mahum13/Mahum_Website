MUSIC

I have been recording as a cover artist for the last 10 years, and began uploading my work on social media at the age of 14. Working with softwares like GarageBand, I began understanding the process of audio recording at a young age. At first I used to play each instrument myself and layer it on GarageBand, but soon began experimenting with electronic music production.
With an interest in videography, I started making music videos for my covers. I mainly worked with editing softwares like iMovie and Adobe Premiere Pro, and worked on my editing and colour grading skills.
Since February 2021, I have been doing livestreams and have been participating in festivals on the global platform Sessions Live by Pandora as well.
